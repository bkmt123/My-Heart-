# Our Dashboard — README
### *A Husband's Promise · Built with Intention*

---

## What This Is

This is a private, faith-forward relationship dashboard built as a single HTML file. It runs entirely in the browser — no internet connection required after opening, no accounts, no servers. Everything you enter is saved locally on the device using browser storage.

It has two modes: **His Side** (for Burhan — planning, tracking, and managing) and **Her Side** (for his wife — interacting, sharing, and seeing what he's written for her). Both sides share the same data, so notes written on His Side are visible to her on Her Side in real time.

---

## How to Open It

1. Download the file `wife-dashboard.html`
2. Double-click it — it opens in any web browser (Chrome, Safari, Edge, Firefox)
3. No installation. No login. No internet needed.

> **Tip:** Bookmark it in your browser or save it to your home screen on mobile for quick access.

---

## Important: Data Storage

All data is saved in your **browser's local storage** on the device you use. This means:

- Data does **not** sync between devices automatically
- If you clear your browser's site data or cache, entries will be lost
- For shared use, both of you should use the **same browser on the same device**, or keep two separate copies of the file

## The Mode Switcher

At the top of the page, just below the header, is a toggle:

| Button | Who It's For |
|---|---|
| 👤 **His Side** | Burhan — full editing access, planning tools, and the locked Surprises tab |
| 🌸 **Her Side** | His wife — her interactive space, her wishlist, her mood log, and messages from him |

The mode is remembered between sessions. When handing the device to her, tap **Her Side** first.

## Tabs & Features

### Overview
The home screen. Shows a rotating daily ayah, a live love language score, prayer count, dates planned, and gift ideas pending.

**His Side** — Shows a daily reminder prompt (e.g. *"Make du'a for her before she wakes up"*) and fields to write his daily intention for her and something he appreciates about her.

**Her Side** — Shows a personal daily message written to her, and displays whatever he wrote in his intention and appreciation fields as read-only.

---

### Love Languages
Tracks acts of love across all 5 love languages with a bar chart that fills throughout the day.

**His Side** — Tap **＋** next to any language to log an act. Set her primary and secondary love language.

**Her Side** — Views the bars (read-only). Has a field to tell him what kind of love she needs most that week.

---

### Ibadah Together
Tracks the 5 daily prayers prayed together. Includes a morning dhikr streak counter and a field for a personal dua written for her.

**His Side** — Check off each salah, increment the dhikr streak, and write the dua.

**Her Side** — Sees the prayer log and his dua displayed to her in a styled Arabic font area.

---

### Date Nights
Add date ideas, planned dates, and completed dates. Move them through statuses (Idea → Planned → Done). Includes a bucket list area and a preferences field.

**Her Side** — Can edit the preferences field ("What I love — so he knows") and the bucket list. Cannot add or remove dates.

---

### Gifts
Track gift ideas with category (Everyday Joy, Occasion, Experience, Spiritual, Surprise) and budget. Mark gifts as bought.

**His Side** — Full add/remove/mark access. Important Dates tracker with auto-countdown.

**Her Side** — Has a **Wishlist** she can add to herself. She cannot see the gift list details (so surprises stay surprises). She can edit the "My favorites" field so he always knows her preferences.

---

### Check-In
Log how she's feeling with 8 mood options and a note about what she needs that day.

**His Side** — Logs his observation. Sees a list of reflection questions to ask her. Sees her message to him (read-only).

**Her Side** — Logs her own mood and what she needs from him. Has a private message field that only he sees on his side.

Both sides' check-ins are logged with a "Her / Him" label and a timestamp in the history.

---

### Life Vision
A shared long-term space. Includes a marriage milestones timeline and three note areas: marriage vision, her goals, and an unsent letter.

Both sides can write in all fields — this is a shared space for both of them.

---

## Data That Carries Across Sides

These notes are written on one side and visible on the other:

| Written by Him (His Side) | Visible to Her (Her Side) |
|---|---|
| Daily intention for her | "A message from your husband" |
| Daily appreciation | "He appreciates this about you today" |
| Dua for her | Displayed in Arabic-style text |
| Gift list | Hidden (she sees wishlist only) |

| Written by Her (Her Side) | Visible to Him (His Side) |
|---|---|
| Message to him (Check-In tab) | "Her message to me" |
| Wishlist items | Visible in Gifts tab |
| Her love language need | Visible in Love Languages tab |

## Tips for Best Use

**For Burhan:**
- Write your daily intention and appreciation every morning — she'll see it when she opens her side
- Use the Surprises tab to plan 1–2 things per month, even small ones
- Log a love language act every time you do something intentional — the bar is motivating
- Set her birthday and your anniversary in Important Dates so you never miss them
- Keep the dhikr streak going — it's a small thing that builds a big habit

**For her:**
- Add to the wishlist anytime something comes to mind — he checks it
- Use the Check-In tab freely — writing how you feel is never too much
- The "My favorites" field in Gifts is her chance to make sure he always knows her preferences
- The Life Vision tab is a shared space — write in it together
